Located in front of the BMW Group headquarters, BMW Welt unites the past and present under one roof. Learn all about the BMW brand at the BMW Museum and experience all the brands and products of the BMW Group – including BMW, MINI, Rolls-Royce Motor Cars and BMW Motorrad – at BMW Welt. Meanwhile at the BMW Group Plant, you can enjoy exclusive insights into vehicle manufacturing. Experience the BMW Group with all your senses: our fascinating exhibitions, events, tours, and offers guarantee lasting memories for all.
